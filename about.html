About NR Modi & Associates

A law chambers dedicated to principled advocacy, clear advice, and effective dispute resolution.

The Chambers

NR Modi & Associates is a Bharuch-based law firm led by Nirav Modi, Advocate | LLM | FCIArb (London). The chambers advises and represents clients in a wide spectrum of legal matters, from individual disputes to complex commercial and institutional litigation and arbitration.

The firm is built on three pillars: integrity in advice, precision in drafting and pleadings, and persistence in representation. Every brief is handled with personal attention from Advocate Nirav Modi, ensuring that strategy, preparation and execution are aligned with the client’s objectives.

About Advocate Nirav Modi

Nirav Modi is an Advocate enrolled in Gujarat with advanced qualifications including LLM and Fellowship of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (FCIArb), London. He has served as Additional Public Prosecutor and Assistant Government Pleader for the Government of Gujarat, bringing valuable experience from both sides of the bar.

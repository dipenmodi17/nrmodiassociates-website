About NR Modi & Associates

Dedicated to principled advocacy, strategic legal advice and effective dispute resolution.

The Chambers

NR Modi & Associates is a Bharuch-based law chambers led by Advocate Nirav Modi, LLM, FCIArb (London). The firm provides representation and advisory services across civil, criminal, commercial and arbitration matters, serving individuals, businesses and institutions.

The chambers are built on three core principles: Integrity in advice, precision in drafting, and persistence in advocacy. Each matter receives personal attention, ensuring that legal strategy, documentation and representation align with the client’s objectives.

About Advocate Nirav Modi

Nirav Modi is an Advocate enrolled in Gujarat with advanced academic and professional credentials including LLM and Fellowship of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (FCIArb), London.

He has served as Additional Public Prosecutor and Assistant Government Pleader for the Government of Gujarat, providing valuable experience from both prosecution and defence perspectives.

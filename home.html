About the Chambers

NR Modi & Associates is a law chambers based in Bharuch, Gujarat led by Nirav Modi – Advocate | LLM | FCIArb (London).

The chambers provides legal representation and advisory services in civil, criminal, commercial and arbitration matters.

Learn More

Advocate Nirav Modi is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (London) and has served as Additional Public Prosecutor and Assistant Government Pleader for the Government of Gujarat.

The firm focuses on precise legal strategy, ethical advocacy and clear legal advice for individuals and institutions.

About the Chambers

NR Modi & Associates is a law chambers based in Bharuch, Gujarat, led by Nirav Modi, Advocate | LLM | FCIArb (London).

The chambers focuses on delivering precise, practical and ethical legal solutions across civil, criminal, commercial and arbitration matters.

Advocate Nirav Modi is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (London) and has previously served as Additional Public Prosecutor and Assistant Government Pleader for the Government of Gujarat.

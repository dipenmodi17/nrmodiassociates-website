About the Chambers

NR Modi & Associates is a law firm based in Bharuch, Gujarat, led by Nirav Modi, Advocate | LLM | FCIArb (London). The chambers focus on delivering precise, practical and ethical legal solutions across civil, criminal, commercial and arbitration matters.

Nirav Modi is a Certified Arbitrator and Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (CIArb), London, and has previously served as Additional Public Prosecutor and Assistant Government Pleader for the Government of Gujarat. This unique blend of public service and private practice experience allows the firm to handle complex and sensitive disputes with confidence.

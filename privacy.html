Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how NR Modi & Associates ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or interact with the chambers in connection with legal services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Contact us through our website
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Request a consultation or meeting
  • Engage us for legal or arbitration services

2. Types of Information

The personal information we may collect includes:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone, address)
  • Occupation and organization (where relevant)
  • Case-related documents and information you choose to share
  • Communication preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our legal and arbitration services
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Communicate with you about your matters and appointments
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy or as required by law or professional obligations.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, consistent with our professional duties as legal practitioners.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic.

7. Your Rights

You may have rights under applicable law to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
